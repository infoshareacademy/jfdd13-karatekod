GAME INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the BookSwapp game!

In this game, your aim is to catch books falling from the sky. Each book caught gives a point. If you miss the book, it will make a hole in the footbridge. If you catch the bomb or fall down into the whole, the game is over

How to play?

Use to move your player left and right and to jump. The more points you collect, the faster books fall. Good luck with catching them all!

Enjoy!
