Welcome to BookSwapp game!

In this game, you have to catch books dropping from the sky. Catch as many books as you can. Beware! Dont miss any book or you loose.

How to play?

Use arrows to control cart. You get 1 point for every caught book. If you miss a book, you loose. Be careful! With each level, more books are falling from the sky. Try to catch them all!

Enjoy!
